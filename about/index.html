About Tahsina Saosun

I am a Front End Software Engineer with a passion for building inclusive and high-performance user experiences. My expertise lies in React, TypeScript, JavaScript (ES6+), and CSS/HTML, with a strong focus on Web Accessibility (WCAG 2.1) and performance optimization.

During my tenure at Morgan Stanley and E*TRADE, I've led frontend migrations, architected reusable component libraries, and collaborated with cross-functional teams to deliver critical trading and financial platforms to hundreds of thousands of users.

I am interested in the intersection of computer science with finance, social impact, and the arts. I believe that accessible and well-designed technology is a powerful tool for positive change.
